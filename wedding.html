About Our Wedding

We wanted to keep it simple...Hawaiian simple.

We have decided for a very simple wedding with our closest friends and family members.

The ceremony will be at Kukahiko Estate, a very scenic location in the wonderful island of Maui (Hawaii).
There will be traditional Hawaiian food, drinks and music to relax in a perfect Islands atmosphere.
If you are not getting married or being a bridesmaid/groomsman, feel free to come in semi formal or island attire.
